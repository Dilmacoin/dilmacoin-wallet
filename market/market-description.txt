Have your Dilmacoins always with you, in your pocket! You pay by quickly scanning a QR-code. As a merchant, you receive payments reliably and instantly. Dilmacoin Wallet is the first mobile Dilmacoin app, and arguably also the most secure!

FEATURES:

• No registration, web service or cloud needed! This wallet is de-centralized and peer to peer.
• Display of Dilmacoin amount in HUE and mHUE.
• Conversion to and from national currencies.
• Sending and receiving of Dilmacoin via NFC, QR-codes or Dilmacoin URLs.
• Address book for regularly used Dilmacoin addresses.
• When you're offline, you can still pay via Bluetooth.
• System notification for received coins.
• App widget for Dilmacoin balance.

You can find more about Dilmacoin at
http://dilmacoin.org

If you want to contribute to Dilmacoin Wallet, the project is located at
https://github.com/schildbach/dilmacoin-wallet-new
